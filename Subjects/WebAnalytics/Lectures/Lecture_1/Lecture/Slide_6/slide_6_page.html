How Much Data is There ?

It’s estimated that the world creates 2.5 quintillion bytes of data every day. That’s a big “wow factor”!

  • People make 3 billion minutes of calls on Skype per day.
  • People share 5 billion Snapchat videos and photos per day.
  • People send 333.2 billion emails per day.
  • People spend 1 million USD per minute online
